## About the Digital Twin Consortium

The [Digital Twin Consortium](https://www.digitaltwinconsortium.org/) drives the awareness, adoption, interoperability, and development of digital twin technology. As a program of Object Management Group (OMG), DTC brings together industry, government, and academia to collaborate on best practices and frameworks.

**Note:** DTC creates frameworks and best practices, not standards. The consortium enables industry collaboration and knowledge sharing to advance digital twin technology adoption.
